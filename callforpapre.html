    Call for Papers

    Abstracts should be submitted via email to 10iygec2025@gmail.com no later than October 10th, 2024. Papers may have joint authorship with senior professionals; however, the first and presenting author must be a young engineer who is one of the authors. The abstract should include the chosen theme, as well as contact information such as email address and phone number for the corresponding author.

    Selected papers will undergo peer review for possible publication in the IGJ Journal

    Important Dates

    Important Dates Date
    Date of abstract submission starts 10th October 2024
    Date of abstract submission deadline 31st October 2024
    Intimation of accepted abstract and last date of registration 2nd November 2024
    Full-length paper submission deadline 5th January 2025
    Intimation of paper acceptance 10th January 2025
    Conference date 7th and 8th March 2025
